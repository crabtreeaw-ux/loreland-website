LORELAND

Loreland is a location-aware storytelling app that creates original horror stories inspired by your immediate surroundings. Open the app anywhere, press play, and hear an eerie tale that unfolds right where you're standing. Waiting for your coffee. Walking through your neighborhood. Sitting in the break room. Resting at a highway pull-off. Even the most familiar places can become strangely unfamiliar.
