start here

Loreland is a location-aware storytelling app that creates original horror stories inspired by your immediate surroundings. Open the app anywhere, press play, and hear an eerie tale that unfolds right where you're standing. Waiting for your coffee. Walking through your neighborhood. Sitting in the break room. Resting at a highway pull-off. Even the most familiar places can become strangely unfamiliar. Each story is uniquely crafted using your hyper-local environment as inspiration, transforming ordinary moments into unsettling encounters filled with mystery, uncanny events, and lingering suspense.

Narrative Augmented Reality (Narrative AR) is a new form of storytelling that augments the world not with digital graphics, but with imagination. Instead of placing virtual objects on a screen, Loreland layers AI-generated narratives onto the real places around you, using your location, the surrounding landscape, weather, time of day, and atmosphere as the foundation for each story. The physical world becomes the stage, while your imagination does the rest. A quiet neighborhood, a parking lot, or the trail you walk every day suddenly carries mystery, tension, and possibility. By transforming familiar places into living settings for immersive stories, Narrative AR introduces a new way to experience both storytelling and the world itself.

How does it work?

  1. Allow location access.
  2. Choose a horror genre.
  3. Listen to a story inspired by where you're standing.

Do I need to download anything?

No.

Will I hear the same story twice?

No. Every story is generated uniquely for your location, so each listening experience is different.

How long is each story?

About two minutes.

Do I have to be somewhere spooky?

No. Loreland works anywhere — from a grocery store to an airport, neighborhood park, office building, or hiking trail.

Every response is read by the creator.

Your feedback directly shapes what Loreland becomes next. The short questionnaire below only takes a minute, and it's the single most helpful thing you can do to help this grow into a fully-featured app.
